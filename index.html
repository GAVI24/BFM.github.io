Today's Article

The Kingdom Of God

Matthew 6:33

Seek ye First The Kingdom of God and His righteousness and all things shall be given to you

This is the gist of the matter of life. "Get to know your master, determine what he wants and do it.

On Earth God created man that he might serve Him. In disobedience man ends up not serving the master (God) and therefore man finds himself in need. Our Lord Jesus Christ continues to indicate how important seeking the Kingdom is in chapter 13:45. It's like a man looking for treasure and once he finds the Kingdom of God he forgets about everything else.
